Title:Barry White....
Author:marcin
When:31 Mar 2014
Taxonomy:English,English blog,poem,książka 4
SkipMain:true

<hr size="2" noshade="noshade" width="60%" align="center" style="color: rgb(0, 64, 128);"><div align="center"><font size="1">Wiersz w nowszej wersji pojawił się w książce <a href=?q=taxonomy/term/książka+4>Gdy na was patrzę</a></font></div><hr size="2" noshade="noshade" width="60%" align="center" style="color: rgb(0, 64, 128);">
<p>I will say yes to you,<br />
but let me in thru<br />
and let me just know,<br />
that I can now come,</p>
<p>And I will love here<br />
and make us hot there<br />
and hold you in arms<br />
with all your charms,</p>
<p>I will say yes to you,<br />
but let me in thru<br />
and let me just know,<br />
that I can now come,</p>
<p>And I will kiss here<br />
and feel you there<br />
and hold you with arms<br />
with all your charms,</p>
<p>I will say yes to you,<br />
but let me in thru<br />
and let me just know,<br />
that I can now come,</p>
<!--break-->
<p>Just show me it tight<br />
each day and night....</p>
