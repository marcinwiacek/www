Title:Show me heaven
Author:marcin
When:09 Jun 2014
Taxonomy:English,English blog,poem,książka 4
SkipMain:true

<hr size="2" noshade="noshade" width="60%" align="center" style="color: rgb(0, 64, 128);"><div align="center"><font size="1">Wiersz w nowszej wersji pojawił się w książce <a href=?q=taxonomy/term/książka+4>Gdy na was patrzę</a></font></div><hr size="2" noshade="noshade" width="60%" align="center" style="color: rgb(0, 64, 128);">
<p>Hey baby, I need your hand,<br />
help me and understand,<br />
I'm frightened more than you,<br />
though I'm barely touching you,<br />
you've such amazing grace...</p>
<p>Hey baby, show me heaven today,<br />
shake me like yesterday,<br />
come over here above it all,<br />
help me before next fall,<br />
you've such amazing grace...</p>
<p>Hey baby, hold me so tight,<br />
hold me morning, day, night,<br />
I've shivers down my spine<br />
and it feels so divine,<br />
you've such amazing grace...</p>
